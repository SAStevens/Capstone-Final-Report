About Old Dogs-New Tricks



Old Dogs-New Tricks is a simple, straightforward blog website where people can gather to look, learn, and leave a little more informed about software development topics than when they arrived.

At this time only registered users are able to create new post, ask questions, leave comments, or provide answers to existing posts. But signing up is quick, free and easy!

Want to validate your opinion? Verify a hunch? Or just ask a simple question to find out what those around you think about matters pertaining to software devlopement, building websites, user interfaces, and user experiences.

The key is to keep things simple, blog post, comments, questions, and answers should avoid using technical language and industry jargon.

As always, inciting violence, using profanity, threatening or disrespecting others is strictly prohibited and will result in the immediate removal and permanent banning of your account.
